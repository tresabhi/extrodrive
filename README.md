# Extrodrive

Casing for turning an NVMe M.2 2280 SSD into an external one, supporting USB 3.2 and transfers up to 1.25 GB/s.

## Assembling

Lay the top casing upside down and insert the heat sink into the slot. Apply the thermal pad roughly to the center of the heat sink.

<img src="https://i.imgur.com/UrPUPWB.png" width="512" />

Separate from the previous assembly, insert the SSD into the PH80-583S.

<img src="https://i.imgur.com/4mVAVRB.png" width="512" />

Flip the second assembly upside down and place it inside the previously assembled assembly, with the top casing still upside down.

<img src="https://i.imgur.com/vKH7MUs.png" width="512" />

Slide the bottom casing into the rest of the assembly diagonally, matching the notches on both casing halves.

<img src="https://i.imgur.com/ppSShUt.png" width="512" />
